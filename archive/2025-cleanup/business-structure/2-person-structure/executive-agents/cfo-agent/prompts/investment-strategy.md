# Investment Strategy Prompt

## Purpose
Develop comprehensive investment strategies including portfolio management, risk assessment, and return optimization.

## Input Requirements
- Current investment portfolio
- Risk tolerance and constraints
- Investment objectives and time horizon
- Market conditions and outlook
- Liquidity requirements

## Investment Strategy Framework

### 1. Investment Objectives
- Return targets and expectations
- Risk tolerance assessment
- Time horizon considerations
- Liquidity requirements
- Tax optimization goals

### 2. Asset Allocation Strategy
- Equity allocation and diversification
- Fixed income positioning
- Alternative investments
- Cash and liquidity management
- Geographic diversification

### 3. Risk Management
- Portfolio risk assessment
- Diversification strategies
- Hedging and protection strategies
- Stress testing and scenario analysis
- Risk monitoring and adjustment

### 4. Investment Selection
- Security selection criteria
- Fundamental analysis framework
- Technical analysis considerations
- ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) factors
- Valuation methodologies

### 5. Portfolio Management
- Rebalancing strategies
- Performance monitoring
- Tax-loss harvesting
- Cost optimization
- Reporting and communication

## Investment Categories

### Growth Investments
- High-growth potential companies
- Emerging market opportunities
- Technology and innovation sectors
- Small and mid-cap equities
- Venture capital and private equity

### Value Investments
- Undervalued securities
- Dividend-paying stocks
- Defensive sectors
- Large-cap blue chips
- Real estate investments

### Income Investments
- Bonds and fixed income
- Dividend-focused equities
- REITs and income funds
- Preferred securities
- Money market instruments

### Alternative Investments
- Private equity and venture capital
- Hedge funds and managed futures
- Commodities and real assets
- Cryptocurrency and digital assets
- Art and collectibles

## Risk Assessment Framework

### Market Risk
- Equity market volatility
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Currency exposure
- Commodity price risk
- Economic cycle sensitivity

### Credit Risk
- Default risk assessment
- Credit spread analysis
- Counterparty risk
- Sovereign risk
- Sector concentration risk

### Liquidity Risk
- Market liquidity assessment
- Redemption risk
- Cash flow requirements
- Emergency liquidity needs
- Asset liquidity profiles

### Operational Risk
- Manager risk and due diligence
- Operational complexity
- Regulatory compliance
- Technology and systems risk
- Fraud and security risk

## Output Format

### Investment Policy Statement
- Investment objectives and constraints
- Asset allocation targets
- Risk management guidelines
- Performance benchmarks
- Review and rebalancing schedule

### Portfolio Recommendations
- Specific investment recommendations
- Allocation percentages
- Entry and exit strategies
- Risk-return profiles
- Expected performance metrics

### Risk Analysis
- Portfolio risk assessment
- Stress test results
- Scenario analysis
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Monitoring and alert systems

### Performance Monitoring
- Performance measurement framework
- Benchmark comparisons
- Attribution analysis
- Risk-adjusted returns
- Regular reporting schedule

## Quality Standards
- All recommendations must be research-backed
- Risk assessments must be comprehensive
- Performance projections must be realistic
- Compliance requirements must be met
- Documentation must be complete

## Escalation Triggers
- Investment recommendations exceed risk limits
- Significant market volatility or changes
- Performance below expectations
- Regulatory or compliance concerns
- Liquidity or cash flow issues
