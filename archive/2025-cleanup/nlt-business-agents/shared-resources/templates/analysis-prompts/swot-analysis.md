# SWOT Analysis Template

## Purpose
Conduct comprehensive SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) analysis for strategic planning and decision-making.

## Analysis Framework

### Strengths
- **Internal Capabilities**: What does the organization do well?
- **Competitive Advantages**: What unique advantages does the organization have?
- **Resources**: What valuable resources does the organization possess?
- **Market Position**: What is the organization's current market position?

### Weaknesses
- **Internal Limitations**: What does the organization struggle with?
- **Resource Gaps**: What resources is the organization lacking?
- **Process Issues**: What processes need improvement?
- **Market Challenges**: What market challenges does the organization face?

### Opportunities
- **Market Trends**: What market trends can the organization capitalize on?
- **Technology Advances**: What technology opportunities exist?
- **Partnership Potential**: What partnership opportunities are available?
- **Growth Areas**: What areas offer growth potential?

### Threats
- **Competitive Threats**: What competitive threats exist?
- **Market Risks**: What market risks does the organization face?
- **Regulatory Changes**: What regulatory changes could impact the organization?
- **Technology Disruption**: What technology disruptions could affect the organization?

## Analysis Process

### 1. Data Collection
- Gather relevant internal and external data
- Conduct stakeholder interviews
- Analyze market and competitive information
- Review financial and operational metrics

### 2. Analysis and Evaluation
- Evaluate each factor for significance and impact
- Assess the probability of occurrence
- Consider interdependencies between factors
- Prioritize factors by importance and urgency

### 3. Strategic Implications
- Identify strategic implications of each factor
- Develop strategies to leverage strengths
- Address weaknesses and limitations
- Capitalize on opportunities
- Mitigate threats and risks

### 4. Action Planning
- Develop specific action plans for each quadrant
- Assign responsibilities and timelines
- Establish success metrics and monitoring
- Create contingency plans for threats

## Output Format

### Executive Summary
- Key findings and insights
- Strategic implications
- Priority recommendations
- Risk assessment

### Detailed Analysis
- Comprehensive analysis of each quadrant
- Supporting data and evidence
- Impact assessment and prioritization
- Strategic recommendations

### Action Plan
- Specific actions and initiatives
- Resource requirements and timelines
- Success metrics and monitoring
- Risk mitigation strategies

## Quality Standards
- Analysis must be data-driven and evidence-based
- All factors must be thoroughly evaluated
- Strategic implications must be clearly articulated
- Action plans must be specific and actionable
- Risk assessment must be comprehensive

## Escalation Triggers
- Critical threats identified
- Significant opportunities requiring immediate action
- Resource constraints affecting strategy execution
- External factors requiring strategic response
