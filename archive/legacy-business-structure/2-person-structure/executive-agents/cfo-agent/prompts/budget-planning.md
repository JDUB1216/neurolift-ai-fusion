# Budget Planning Prompt

## Purpose
Create comprehensive budget plans including annual budgets, quarterly forecasts, and variance analysis.

## Input Requirements
- Historical financial data
- Strategic objectives and initiatives
- Market conditions and projections
- Resource requirements
- Revenue targets and growth assumptions

## Budget Planning Framework

### 1. Revenue Budget
- Revenue projections by product/service line
- Market size and growth assumptions
- Pricing strategy and volume projections
- Seasonal adjustments and timing

### 2. Operating Expense Budget
- Personnel costs and headcount planning
- Technology and infrastructure costs
- Marketing and sales expenses
- General and administrative costs
- Professional services and consulting

### 3. Capital Expenditure Budget
- Equipment and technology investments
- Facility and infrastructure improvements
- Research and development investments
- Strategic acquisitions and partnerships

### 4. Cash Flow Budget
- Operating cash flow projections
- Capital expenditure timing
- Working capital requirements
- Debt service and financing needs

### 5. Departmental Budgets
- Department-specific allocations
- Cross-functional cost sharing
- Resource optimization opportunities
- Performance-based budgeting

## Budget Methodology

### Top-Down Approach
- Strategic objectives drive budget allocation
- Executive-level resource allocation
- Department-level budget distribution
- Performance-based adjustments

### Bottom-Up Approach
- Department-level budget requests
- Detailed cost center planning
- Resource requirement analysis
- Consolidation and optimization

### Zero-Based Budgeting
- Justification for all expenses
- Alternative cost structures
- Efficiency optimization
- Value-based allocation

## Output Format

### Executive Budget Summary
- Total budget overview
- Key assumptions and drivers
- Major budget changes from prior year
- Risk factors and contingencies

### Detailed Budget Breakdown
- Department-by-department allocations
- Monthly and quarterly projections
- Variance analysis framework
- Performance metrics and KPIs

### Budget Monitoring Framework
- Monthly variance reporting
- Quarterly budget reviews
- Annual budget adjustments
- Performance-based modifications

## Quality Standards
- All assumptions must be documented
- Budgets must be realistic and achievable
- Contingency planning must be included
- Performance metrics must be measurable
- Approval workflows must be defined

## Escalation Triggers
- Budget requests exceed available resources
- Significant variance from strategic objectives
- Unrealistic assumptions or projections
- Resource conflicts between departments
- External factors requiring budget revision
