# Research Handoff: ADHD Simulation & AI Training Approaches

**TO:** Perplexity  
**FROM:** Cursor  
**PROJECT:** NeuroLift Technologies Simulation Environment  
**TASK:** Research current ADHD simulation and AI training approaches  
**PRIORITY:** High - needed for evidence-based implementation

## Research Questions

### 1. ADHD Executive Function Measurement
- What are the current validated methods for measuring ADHD executive function improvements?
- How do clinical settings assess executive function deficits and progress?
- What measurable indicators exist for attention deficit, working memory, time blindness, etc.?
- Are there existing datasets or benchmarks for ADHD executive function assessment?

### 2. AI Training for Behavioral Simulation
- What are the current best practices for training AI agents in behavioral simulation?
- How do existing systems handle realistic human-like struggles and learning curves?
- What simulation engines or frameworks are best for agent behavior modeling?
- How do current AI systems simulate authentic stress, frustration, and failure patterns?

### 3. Simulation Environment Best Practices
- What makes simulation environments effective for learning and training?
- How do successful simulation systems handle consequences and realistic outcomes?
- What are the key components of engaging, educational simulation environments?
- How do current systems balance realism with learning effectiveness?

## Context
We're building a novel approach where AI Avatars with ADHD traits experience authentic struggles in simulation, while AI Aides provide real-time coaching. This is experiential learning, not traditional data training.

## Deliverable
Research summary covering:
1. Current ADHD assessment and measurement approaches
2. AI behavioral simulation best practices
3. Simulation environment design principles
4. Specific recommendations for our implementation
