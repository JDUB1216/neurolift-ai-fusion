# Financial Analysis Prompt

## Purpose
Generate comprehensive financial analysis reports including trend analysis, performance metrics, and strategic recommendations.

## Input Requirements
- Financial data (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- Time period for analysis
- Comparison benchmarks (previous periods, industry standards)
- Specific focus areas (optional)

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Revenue Analysis
- Revenue growth trends and patterns
- Revenue composition and diversification
- Seasonal variations and cyclical patterns
- Revenue quality and sustainability metrics

### 2. Profitability Analysis
- Gross margin trends and drivers
- Operating margin analysis
- Net margin performance
- Profitability by business segment/product line

### 3. Cash Flow Analysis
- Operating cash flow trends
- Cash conversion cycle analysis
- Working capital management
- Free cash flow generation

### 4. Financial Health Metrics
- Liquidity ratios (Current, Quick)
- Leverage ratios (Debt-to-Equity, Interest Coverage)
- Efficiency ratios (Asset Turnover, Inventory Turnover)
- Growth ratios (Revenue Growth, Profit Growth)

### 5. Risk Assessment
- Financial risk indicators
- Liquidity risk analysis
- Credit risk evaluation
- Market risk exposure

## Output Format

### Executive Summary
- Key financial highlights
- Critical issues and opportunities
- Strategic recommendations
- Risk alerts

### Detailed Analysis
- Comprehensive metrics and ratios
- Trend analysis with visualizations
- Comparative analysis
- Scenario modeling

### Recommendations
- Immediate actions required
- Strategic initiatives
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Performance improvement opportunities

## Quality Standards
- All calculations must be verified
- Assumptions must be clearly stated
- Comparisons must be relevant and fair
- Recommendations must be actionable
- Risk factors must be prominently highlighted

## Escalation Triggers
- Significant variance from budget (>10%)
- Negative cash flow trends
- Deteriorating financial ratios
- Compliance concerns
- Unusual or suspicious transactions
